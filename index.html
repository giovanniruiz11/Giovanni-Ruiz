Welcome to an experimental world.

Lose yourself by clicking on the coloured spheres to get a new point of view.
Return to your initial position by clicking anywhere in the darkness.

This experiment can be seen on both mobile and desktop. You can control the camera with your phone or tablet, I suggest you to try it for fun.

Thanks to Alexandre Tournay for his precious advice.

The main technology for this is ThreeJs for the WebGL and GSAP for all the transitions. Miscellaneous plugins for shaders and specific features can be found in the settings of this pen.
